Market- Product Focus

Our goal for the Sunny Sled is to provide vacationers and beach goers a necessary resource for all of their trips to the ocean.

We hope our product will one day be on everyone’s vacation checklist as an essential item to stow in the back of the car or purchase once you arrive from local beach shops.

Our target market is, of course, the travel and beach gear markets.
Our Sunny Sled will serve as a direct complement to items like beach chairs, umbrellas and other supplies.

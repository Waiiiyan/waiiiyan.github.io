Market- Product Focus

Our goal for the Sunny Sled is to provide vacationers and beach goers a necessary resource for all of their trips to the ocean.

We hope our product will one day be on everyone’s vacation checklist as an essential item to stow in the back of the car or purchase once you arrive from local beach shops.

Our target market is, of course, the travel and beach gear markets.
Our Sunny Sled will serve as a direct complement to items like beach chairs, umbrellas and other supplies. 

  1. Target Custmer  - We will target numerous different segments within the possible demographics of people who may be interested in Sunny Sled. Our largest and most important segment is the family demographic. This group will drive the majority of our sales as beach trips are most common among families and families have more things to carry than the normal person. Additionally, we hope to have some portion of our marketing targeted toward college students. The Sunny Sled will be great for large groups of friends who plan to hit the beach. Whether its a weekend trip or spring break, our built in cooler will be immensely useful for these sort of customers. Finally, senior citizens are another essential demographic that could definitely be in the market for what we have to offer. Increased accessibility, added refrigerant and increased storage all cater towards possible needs that senior citizens may be in search for when they go to the beach.
  2. Sale Period - We hope to eventually capture other markets such as other outdoor markets. Once we reach a comfortable of success in the beach and travel market, we hope to not settle, and continue to expand. Our sled will also work great in winter environments as well and could eventually break into the camping and outdoors supplies area. Sunny Sled could be marketed during our “offseason” eventually. This could greatly grow our company to have a huge advantage over our competitors. Rarely can a beach item translate to the winter environment as well. Outdoor campers can use our product to easily and efficiently bring items to their lodge, cabin, or campsite and can easily hold an of the numerous items required for such trips.
  3. Our Goals  - What sets us apart from competitors is we eliminate some of the main issues with a normal beach cart. This includes eliminating the hassle from normal beach crossings and providing extra storage for food and supplies. Essentially, what we offer is an all-in-one product that can be purchased at the same price as the competitors but with so much more. Vacationers and beachgoers can walk away that made a nobel investment that will make the whole family or group happy. Also, with potential to expand into the winter market, we off our consumers a massive increase on their return on investment. No longer will they feel guilty about buying something that they can only use for one season out of the year. The multi-use capacity of our product will draw in those customers who are looking for the most value.

Financial Data and Projection


 Financial Data:  In order to calculate our Break Even Point, we decided to estimate our Variable Cost based on 2020-Q1. Our variable cost changes from quarter to quarter as we change seasons. This is expected since we are not expecting our consumers to buy our product during winter seasons, however, we note that there is in increase in sales during warmer months which falls between Quarter 2 and Quarter 3 of each year. These variable costs change due to economies of scale and larger/smaller batch ordering.
Project Organzation: As our company starts, we are going to implement a an organization structure with a CEO and 5 Vice Presidents. Our VP of Marketing and Communications will hold 5 management positions including Sales, Digital Marketing, Product/Brand Management, Market Research, and Advertising/Promotions. Our Product and Brand Manager will have an associate Product and Brand Manager and they will have other Marketing Assistants. Marketing Assistants will help with general brand managements and will also help the Digital Marketing Manager to work on social media and email campaigning. Our VP of Human Resources will eventually have a team that will work on payroll, talent acquisition, and training and development of incoming employees. Our VP of Accounting and Finance will soon have a director of Finance that will assist our VP to ensure that general accounting and spending it done with the future of the company in mind. Product Engineers, Operations Analyst, and Technicians will be hired to aid the Vice President of Operations as we begin developing our product. This department will work closely with the Vice President of Research and Development. The Department of Research and Development will hold positions including Researchers and Product Testers.
